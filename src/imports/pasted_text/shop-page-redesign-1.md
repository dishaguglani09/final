Design and develop a premium, modern, highly interactive Shop Page for an organic food / farm-fresh ecommerce brand.
The current shop page feels basic, static, and outdated. Redesign it into a visually immersive, conversion-focused shopping experience with premium product discovery, elegant filtering, smooth animations, and modern ecommerce interactions.

The design should feel like:

A luxury organic marketplace
Modern artisanal ecommerce
Fresh, trustworthy, premium, handcrafted

The UI must feel polished, dynamic, responsive, and emotionally engaging — NOT like a generic ecommerce template.
