## 🎨 Design System

### **Color Palette**
- **Primary**: `#4a6741` (Muted green)
- **Accent**: `#8b9d83` (Sage green)
- **Background**: `#fdfcfb` (Warm white)
- **Muted**: `#f5f1ed` (Soft beige)

### **Typography**
- **Headings**: Playfair Display (serif)
- **Body**: Inter (sans-serif)
- **Font Sizes**: Base 16px, up to 8xl for hero

### **Effects**
- **Glassmorphism**: `backdrop-blur-xl` with white/80 opacity
- **Shadows**: Layered from sm to 2xl
- **Gradients**: Linear and radial for depth
- **Rounded Corners**: 2xl to 3xl for premium feel

### **Animations**
- **Spring**: For natural motion (stiffness: 200-300, damping: 15-30)
- **Ease Out**: For smooth transitions
- **Duration**: 200ms to 600ms for most effects
- **Stagger**: 50-100ms delays for list items
